## Architecture-First Workflow

1. **Define** architecture in `.pi/architecture/modules/*.md`
2. **Plan** epic with `/architect --epic "Name"`
3. **Implement** with `/pipeline` (auto-validates at every step)
4. **Harden** — every MR must pass 10 CI stages
5. **Close** epic with architecture readiness (runbook, DR, docs)
